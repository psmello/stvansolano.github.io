Introducing #XamarinDiplomado, free and online mobile application development content for Latin America audiences

#XamarinDiplomado is a free and online course developed by Xamarin & Microsoft MVPs Esteban Solano, Humberto Jaimes and Enrique Aguilar focused on mobile application development with C# and .Net in collaboration with a awesome team of Microsoft folks as a way of bring and teach about mobile development for Latin America audiences.

More about the diplomado

It was started in 2016 with a response from the region and the local .Net communities in Latin America after releasing a 100% Spanish online course covering Android, iOS and Windows with C# and Xamarin.

The content is delivered in a set of modules released weekly covering topics including the traditional approach of mobile development for Android / iOS / Forms, covering the native concepts of each mobile platform featuring conected apps, database, XAML, Xamarin.Forms and Microsoft Azure for the creation of applications connected to the cloud.
